The System

4 Phases to a Predictable Patient Pipeline

A complete, sequenced system — each phase builds on the last until your census grows automatically, month after month.

01
Acquisition Phase

Create a Direct Stream of Qualified Patients

We deploy precision-targeted paid campaigns on the world's largest social platforms — reaching families actively searching for home care, not passive scrollers. Every dollar of ad spend is directed at high-intent consumers who are ready to make a care decision now, not later. Your agency gets in front of them first, consistently.

  • Meta & Facebook Ads
  • Google Search & Display
  • High-intent audience targeting
  • HIPAA-compliant ad creative
  • Weekly campaign optimization
02
Conversion Phase

Capture, Engage & Filter Every Prospect

Traffic alone means nothing without a system to catch it. We install conversion-engineered landing pages built specifically for home care, backed by AI intake agents that respond in under 90 seconds — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Every lead is engaged instantly via SMS and email sequences, then filtered so only qualified prospects reach your team.

  • Custom landing pages
  • AI intake bot (24/7)
  • Automated SMS sequences
  • Email nurture campaigns
  • CRM pipeline automation
03
Predictability Phase

Turn 1 Patient Into 2 — Automatically

Once a patient is under your care, the system keeps working. We deploy structured Google review campaigns that build your 5-star authority and drive organic inbound traffic. Simultaneously, a referral incentive program turns satisfied families, discharge planners, and physicians into a steady source of warm referrals — compounding your census growth without additional ad spend.

  • Google review generation
  • 5-star reputation management
  • Referral incentive program
  • Professional referral outreach
  • Organic traffic growth
04
Continuity Phase

Keep Your Pipeline Active — Quarter After Quarter

Your existing database is a dormant goldmine. Every quarter, we launch targeted reactivation campaigns via email and SMS — re-engaging cold leads, past inquiries, and long-term prospects who weren't ready before but may be now. This keeps revenue flowing from contacts you already own, without spending a dollar more on acquisition.

  • Quarterly reactivation campaigns
  • Email & SMS re-engagement
  • Dormant lead revival
  • Long-term prospect nurture
  • Pipeline always-on cadence
